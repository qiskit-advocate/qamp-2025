# Worksheet — Bell States (Tier-1)

1) Circle which circuit makes **matching** results (mostly 00/11).  
2) You add **one gate** and the results switch to **opposites** (mostly 01/10).  
   • Which gate? • Where does it go?  
3) Pixel reveal (team task): use your 16 bits and the class cipher to uncover a 4×4 picture.  
   • In 2 sentences, explain your strategy and one change that improved your results.

**Rubric (10):** correct IDs (4), correct gate & placement (3), clear strategy (3).
